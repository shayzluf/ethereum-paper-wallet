How to Use Your Paper Wallet

What is a Paper Wallet?

A paper wallet is a physical document containing your Ethereum public address and private key, allowing you to store Ethereum offline.

Security Best Practices
  • Generate wallets on a secure, offline computer
  • Use a printer directly connected to your computer (not networked)
  • Never save digital copies of your private key
  • Store your paper wallet in a secure, waterproof, and fireproof container
  • Consider making multiple copies and storing them in different secure locations
  • Never share your private key with anyone
How to Store Your Paper Wallet
  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Consider laminating for protection against water damage
  • Keep in a secure location like a safe or safety deposit box
  • Don't fold or damage the QR codes
How to Use Funds from Your Paper Wallet

To use the funds stored on your paper wallet, you'll need to import your private key into an Ethereum wallet software. Be cautious when doing this and ensure you're using a secure device.
